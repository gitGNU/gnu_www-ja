“Happy Birthday to GNU” in your language

We have translated subtitles of the film in over 20 languages, available for you to show on Software Freedom Day or at your local user group meetings.

We're using the SubRip format for subtitles, which is compatible with all the major free software video players.

What player are you using?

        

  • Totem
  • Xine
  • Mplayer
  • VLC
  • Kaffeine

Please send your photos and extra translations to <campaigns@fsf.org>.

Please send general FSF & GNU inquiries to <gnu@gnu.org>. There are also other ways to contact the FSF. Broken links and other corrections or suggestions can be sent to <webmasters@gnu.org>.
