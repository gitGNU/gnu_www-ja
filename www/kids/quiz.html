A Quiz for Kids

Take this quiz and test your knowledge.

  1. What is free software?
    1. Software which is given away to users without them having to pay anything for it.
    2. Software which gives the users access to the source code.
    3. Software which gives the users' freedom to run, copy, distribute, study, change and improve the software.
  2. What is Linux?
    1. An operating system.
    2. A kernel.
    3. A penguin.
  3. What is GNU?
    1. An operating system.
    2. An animal.
    3. A license.
  4. What is piracy?
    1. Robbery committed at sea.
    2. Copyright infringement.
    3. To download music and films and share them with your neighbour.

The correct answers are avalible.
