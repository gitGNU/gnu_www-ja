Links to Other Free Software Sites for Kids

Here are some links to other free software sites for kids.

  • Tux Paint

    Tux Paint is a free, award-winning drawing program for children ages 3 to 12 (preschool and K-6). It combines an easy-to-use interface, fun sound effects, and an encouraging cartoon mascot who guides children as they use the program.

  • KDE Kids Games

    Information about the Kids Games included in KDE. All games listed here are part of the kdegames package and included in a standard KDE distribution.

  • Childsplay

    Childsplay is a suite of educational games for young children.
