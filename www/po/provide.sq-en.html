What We Provide

What the FSF provides to computer users, above all, is the chance to use a computer in freedom—using free software.

Since 1985 the FSF has sponsored the development of the free software operating system GNU. The GNU/Linux variant of GNU is already widely used. The GNU/Hurd system, based on our GNU Hurd kernel, is ready for wizards and enthusiasts to try. A wide variety of GNU software in general is available for download.

Things you can obtain directly from the FSF include:

  • Associate Membership
  • Corporate Patronage
  • Software
  • Documentation and books on free software philosophy
  • T-shirts and other GNU Gear
