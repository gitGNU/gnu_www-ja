Other Thank GNUs

Thanks to all who have purchased CD-ROMs, manuals, reference cards, and T-shirts from the FSF. Thanks to those who have purchased Deluxe Distributions.

Thanks to all the volunteers listed in GNU's Who and the unlisted and uncounted who have helped the FSF at shows.

Thanks to all who have assigned copyrights to the Free Software Foundation or who distributed their source code under the GNU General Public License. Thanks to all who have contributed documentation, good bug reports, or other useful criticism.

Please send FSF & GNU inquiries to gnu@gnu.org. There are also other ways to contact the FSF.
Please send broken links and other corrections or suggestions to webmasters@gnu.org.
