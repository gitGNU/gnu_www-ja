What is ACM?

ACM is an aerial combat simulator that works under GNU/Linux. The most notable characteristics are:
  1. It works under the X Window system.
  2. It allows multiplayer.
  3. It is extensible (aircrafts and sceneries).
  4. F16 and MIG by default.
  5. Two weapons type: missil and stream cannon.
  6. Reload of weapons and fuel in airport.

How I obtain a copy of ACM?

You can download the latest version sources from the /proyectos/acm/stable directory from the ftp space. Also you can access to the cvs repository, to follow the develop. If you are a Debian user, you can download the .deb package from the Debian official site.
