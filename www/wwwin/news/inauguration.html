Inauguration of Free Software Foundation of India

The inauguration is to be held on 20th of July 2001 at Galaxy Hall, The Residency Tower, Press Road, Trivandrum by Richard Stallman.

Inauguration of FSF India by Richard Stallman
Venue: Galaxy Hall, The Residency Tower, Press Road, Trivandrum
20.07.2001 - 1045 hrs
Address by Richard Stallman on Free Software and the GNU/Linux Operating System
20.07.2001 - 1400 hrs
Address by Tony Stanco, Founder, FreeDevelopers.Net on the Free Software Business Models
Address by Ross Moore, Department of Mathematics, Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia on the On-line Quiz System with Free Software
Presentation by a team of students of Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management--Kerala on Free Software in IT-enabled Education

RMS's talk at College of Engineering, Trivandrum
Session with government officials
