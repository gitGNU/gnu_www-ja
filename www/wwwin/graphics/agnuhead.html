A GNU Head

[ English | Italian | Japanese | Korean ]

A handsome GNU Head with typical beard and smart-looking curled horns. He or she appears to be smiling contentedly with its works as of yet, but it still gazes off into the distance.

We thank the artist of this GNU Head, even though the artist has not yet been positively identified. We believe that the artist is Etienne Suvasa who has done several covers for FSF publications.

This Head of a GNU is the default art for pages on this web site including GNU's home page.

This Head of a GNU is also used on the front of the 1997 GNU T-shirt, as well as on the FSF's letterhead and business cards. It is also on the spine of many GNU Manuals.
