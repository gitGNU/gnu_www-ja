Pictures from Usenix 2001

These pictures were taken by Ellie Young and Geoffrey Knauth at Usenix 2001 where the GNU Project received the lifetime achievement award.

  • Bob Chassell accepting the award (left to right: Clem Cole, Dan Frye, and Bob Chassell).
  • Another picture of Bob Chassell accepting the award (left to right: Yoonho Park, Clem Cole, Andrew Hume, Dan Frye, Bob Chassell).
  • Bob Chassell all alone.
  • Bob Chassell and Lisa M. Goldstein.
  • A few of the thousands of GNU contributors.
  • Bradley M. Kuhn and David Edelsohn.
