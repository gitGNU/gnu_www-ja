How you can help the GNU Project: Hardware

The GNU Project would accept donations of the following hardware. Please send only known working hardware. Please send offers to <hardware-donations@gnu.org>.

  • Modern rackmount server and workstation computers.
  • Monitors that support 1600x1200 or better resolution (or LCD panels).
  • 900MHz or better laptop computers.
  • 10/100 or gigabit managed ethernet switches.
  • Postscript printers, and printers that work with GNU Ghostscript.
  • Uninterruptible power supplies with good batteries.
  • A T1 router and CSU/DSU.

Please send only hardware that works—we don't want to take people away from other work to fix up broken equipment.

Additionally, donations of the following services would be useful:

  • Additional internet connectivity for our Boston office (it must support the BGP4 protocol).
  • Internet colocation space.

You can also help the GNU Project in other ways.

Please send FSF & GNU inquiries to <gnu@gnu.org>. There are also other ways to contact the FSF.
Please send broken links and other corrections or suggestions to <webmasters@gnu.org>.
