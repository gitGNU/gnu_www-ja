Introduction to Taylor UUCP

Taylor UUCP is a free implementation of UUCP and is the standard UUCP used on the GNU system. - If you don't know what UUCP is chances are, nowadays, that you won't need it. - If you do need it, you've just found one of the finest UUCP implementations available.

If you found a bug or have some other question please direct them to taylor-uucp@gnu.org.

Downloading Taylor UUCP

Taylor UUCP can be found on http://ftp.gnu.org/gnu/uucp/ or one of the mirrors.
