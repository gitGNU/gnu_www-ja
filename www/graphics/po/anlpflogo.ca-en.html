An LPF Logo

Three copies of a logo for the League for Programming Freedom. Each depicts a Statue of Liberty holding up a eight inch or 5.25-inch floppy diskette in her right hand, and a magnetic tape reel rests in her left arm.

We thank Etienne Suvasa for drawing this LPF logo.
