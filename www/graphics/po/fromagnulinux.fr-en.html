Fromagnulinux by Denis Trimaille

Denis used the "Laughing Cow" (La vache qui rit) as a starting point for this artwork. He decided to create "The smiling gnu" (Le gnou qui sourit). Many people will recognize the origin of this artwork. It's all about french cheese in small round boxes. The title of this work comes from "From a GNU Linux" inferred from the french title "Fromage GNU/Linux" (Cheese GNU Linux).

Also take a look at Denis his page: http://www.linuxfranch-county.org/artlinux.html#label_fromagnulinux [Archived Page]
