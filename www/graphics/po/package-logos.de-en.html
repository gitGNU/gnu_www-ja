Logos of GNU packages

This page is a collection of official and semi-official logos of GNU packages, sorted in alphabetical order.

Help us complete the list

Many GNU packages don't have logos—if you are skilled enough and willing to contribute logo for a package, please contact the package maintainers and show them your work. If approved, let us know at <webmasters@gnu.org> in order to add it to this page. A list of all GNU packages is available.

Please send general FSF & GNU inquiries to <gnu@gnu.org>. There are also other ways to contact the FSF.
Please send broken links and other corrections or suggestions to <webmasters@gnu.org>.
