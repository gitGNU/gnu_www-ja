Baby GNU

The Baby GNU is an adorable cartoon-like image of but a wee tyke of a GNU, sucking its thumb and snuggling its blanket closely.

We thank Etienne Suvasa for drawing this Baby GNU.

This picture is available in the following formats:

  • jpeg 6k, 10k
  • png 2k, 3k
