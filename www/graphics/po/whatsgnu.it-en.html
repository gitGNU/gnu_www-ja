What's GNU

Two banners enclose a GNU in this image, one reading "What's GNU?" and the other "GNU's Not Unix!". In the center, a face similar to the GNU Head above rests proudly.

We thank Etienne Suvasa for drawing this What's GNU art.

It is used on this web site to indicate pages that describe what GNU is, including:

  • GNU Project
  • the GNU Manifesto (31k characters)
