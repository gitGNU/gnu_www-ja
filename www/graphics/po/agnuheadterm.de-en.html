A GNU Head in Terminal

This is a version of a GNU Head that can be printed into the TTY and XTerm- compatible terminals. Requires UTF-8 encoding to be enabled at at least 72 columns in your terminal.

We thank Mattias Andrée for his Unicode art.

A GNU Head is available in two formats for the TTY and for XTerm-compatible terminals. A screenshot of what it's supposed to look like are also available.

  • GNU/Linux TTY
  • 256-Color XTerm
