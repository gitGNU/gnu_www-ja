The GNU Art Gallery

Beside the art on these GNU Web pages, we at the GNU Project have the art:

  • on the covers of GNU Manuals
  • on GNU T-shirts
  • on the covers of the GNU's Bulletins

We are looking for new graphic designs for t-shirts and other GNU and FSF items to offer in our shop. If you are interested in contributing some good-quality art to help raise free software awareness, please contact <graphics@gnu.org>. Here are some ideas on how we can use artwork.
