A Typing GNU Hacker

This GNU hunches over a microcomputer, no doubt running free software! The GNU is enclosed in an arch, with "GNU's Not Unix" written above, and "Free Software Foundation" below it.

We thank Jamal Hannah for drawing this Typing GNU Hacker.

It was used on the front of an earlier GNU T-shirt sold by the FSF.

It is used on this web site to indicate pages that list people who are helping or have helped the GNU Project, including these pages:

  • GNU's Who - the People of the GNU Project
  • The GNU Webmasters
