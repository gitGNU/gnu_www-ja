The Spirit of Freedom by Denís Fernández Cabrera

[ Català | English | Français ]

Denís Fernández Cabrera made a nice piece of artwork. The drawing shows us Tux with GNU horns and tail. This magic duo will take you to higher hights. Hovering like a spirit above the big bad world.

This color picture is available in the following formats:

  • PNG 10k (a very tiny and low quality picture.), 51k (a medium size and good quality picture.), 125k (a big and good quality picture.)
  • SVG 118k (a gzip'ed file in SVG vector format.)
