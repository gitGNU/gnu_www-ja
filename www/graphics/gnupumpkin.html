GNU Pumpkin

The GNU Pumpkin is a real pumpkin with the GNU Head carved out. This is a work of Jimmy Rustles, done on the occasion of Halloween, to show his appreciation of all that Richard Stallman has done.

We thank Jimmy Rustles for his GNU Pumpkin art.

The GNU Pumpkin is available in the following sizes:

  • 256px
  • 480px
  • 1024px
  • 1864px
