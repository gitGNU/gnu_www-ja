Dog Cartoon

A cute beagle is sitting by his computer, which shows pop-up ads for a yummy bone, a dog toy, and a comfortable-looking doggy bed. These ads are partially covering the text of an article by Richard Stallman, How Much Surveillance Can Democracy Withstand? (only visible in the large picture). The dog is wondering, “How did they find out I'm a dog?”
