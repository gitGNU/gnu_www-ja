Free Software and Education - Table of Contents

Education → Education Contents

The Education section of the GNU Project is about the importance of using and teaching exclusively Free Software in all educational institutions. It presents articles on the subject, cases of success in implementing Free Software in schools and universities around the world, examples of Free programs used in the classroom to the benefit of teachers and students alike, and Free Software education projects carried out by organizations and governments worldwide.
