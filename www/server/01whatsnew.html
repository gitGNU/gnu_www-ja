October 2001

24 October 2001
For a little while, added a link to the Anti-DMCA Petition from GNUs Flashes on home.html.

22 October 2001
Put out a press release regarding Version 21 of the GNU Emacs Editing Environment.

13 October 2001
Richard Stallman wrote a letter to the editor of Dr. Dobb's Journal in June to clarify the distinction between the free software and open source movements; it is now available for reading in our philosophy section.

12 October 2001
Put out a press release regarding the seventh major release of GNU Bayonne.

The Free Software Foundation and Finite State Machine Labs Inc. (FSMLabs) have announced the release of the Open RTLinux patent license Version 2 fully compliant with the Free Software Foundation's General Public License (GPL). The Open Patent License grants the right to use U.S. Patent No. 5,995,745 in GPL free software without payment of a royalty. This license protects GPL use of the RTLinux process. For more information, please see the updated press release.
