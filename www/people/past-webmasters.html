GNU's Webmasters Past and Present

These people are, or have been at some stage, webmasters of www.gnu.org. If you see something you would like added to, or changed, on this web site, please contact us at webmasters@gnu.org send other questions to gnu@gnu.org). We are always looking for comments and are open to suggestions.
